    RoboBloom

    RoboBloom is an automated plant care system designed to optimize plant growth and maintenance by integrating various sensors and control mechanisms.

    Functions

    Obstacle Sensor Integration: Enhances environmental awareness by detecting and navigating around obstacles.

    Algorithm Development: Processes data from light sensors in real time to determine the direction of the nearest sunlight source.

    Precise Plant Movement: Autonomous and precise movement of potted plants with obstacle avoidance capabilities.

    Motion Control System Implementation: Uses Arduino Nano V3 and L293D Motor Driver for precise rotational and translational movements.

    Automatic Watering System: Monitors soil moisture levels and initiates the watering process autonomously.

    Anita

    Anita is an AI chatbot designed to answer any question related to ICCT, providing students and faculty with instant access to information about the institution.

    Functions

    Natural Language Processing: Utilizes advanced NLP techniques to understand and respond to questions in a conversational manner.

    Speech-to-Text (STT): Converts spoken questions into text for processing, allowing users to interact with the chatbot using voice.

    Text-to-Speech (TTS): Converts text responses into spoken words, enhancing accessibility and user engagement.

    Real-time Responses: Provides instant and accurate answers to user queries, ensuring a seamless and efficient user experience.
